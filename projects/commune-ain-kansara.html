Project Overview

Developed the official website for the Commune Ain Kansara using WordPress. The platform was designed to centralize communication, provide access to local services, and keep the community updated with news and events.

Key Features

  • News and announcements section with categorized articles
  • Service information for residents (administrative forms, contact info, schedules)
  • Community resources and events calendar
  • Mobile-friendly, responsive design
  • Easy-to-use content management for non-technical staff

Technical Implementation

  • WordPress CMS with custom theme tailored to the commune's branding
  • Integration of plugins for SEO, security, and performance optimization
  • Structured content hierarchy for easy navigation
  • Hosting setup and maintenance for reliable uptime

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Ensuring that non-technical staff could update content easily.
Solution: Customized WordPress dashboard and provided training.

Challenge: Building a professional website under time constraints.
Solution: Used a modular design system and optimized workflows to deliver on time.
