Gestion de Stock et des Demandes – Entraide Nationale

Stock and demand management system for national aid coordination.

2024
8 weeks
Entraide Nationale (Royaume du Maroc)
Laravel MySQL Tailwind PHP Dashboard UI/UX

Project Overview

Built a custom stock and demand management system for Entraide Nationale to optimize distribution of technical aids, equipment, and social coverage. The platform improves visibility, validation, and coordination across multiple organizational levels (Central, Regional, and Provincial).

Key Features

  • Multi-level user management (Directeur Central, Coordinateur Régional, Délégué Provincial)
  • Stock management: validation, transfer, and tracking
  • Demand request management: creation, validation, and monitoring
  • Equipment and aid tracking by type, disability category, and social coverage
  • Real-time dashboards for better decision-making
  • Connection visibility and user activity monitoring

Technical Implementation

  • Laravel + MySQL architecture for robust and scalable backend
  • Tailored role-based access control system
  • Dynamic dashboards with data visualization
  • Responsive UI with a focus on usability for administrators
  • Secure authentication and session management

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Managing complex hierarchy with multiple validation steps.
Solution: Implemented a layered role-based permission system with workflow automation.

Challenge: Handling large stock data efficiently.
Solution: Optimized database queries and introduced caching for faster response times.
