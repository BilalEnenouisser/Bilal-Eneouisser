Project Overview

The Brandenburginstitut Instagram posts design project focused on creating visually appealing and educational content that aligns with the institute's brand identity. The designs incorporate modern graphic elements, educational themes, and engaging visuals to increase social media engagement and brand recognition.

Key Deliverables

  • Series of educational Instagram post templates
  • Branded visual identity consistent across all publications
  • Engaging graphics with clear typography and color hierarchy
  • Content-focused layouts enhancing readability
  • Export-ready assets optimized for social media formats

Design Process

  • Researched the target audience to align design with educational communication needs
  • Defined a visual system integrating brand elements (colors, fonts, logo)
  • Designed post templates for different content types (quotes, infographics, tips)
  • Applied best practices in social media design for engagement and clarity
  • Delivered a consistent package of posts ready for publishing

Challenges & Solutions

  • Challenge: Making educational content visually engaging
  • Solution: Used dynamic layouts, iconography, and color coding to simplify complex information.
  • Challenge: Ensuring brand consistency across many posts
  • Solution: Established a modular template system adaptable for various content types.
