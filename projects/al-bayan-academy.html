Project Overview

The Al Bayan Academy Instagram posts design project focused on creating culturally appropriate and educational content that reflects the academy's Islamic values and educational mission. The designs incorporate traditional Islamic art elements, modern graphic design principles, and engaging educational content to connect with the community.

Key Deliverables

  • Instagram post templates reflecting Islamic values and educational goals
  • Visual consistency across all social media publications
  • Elegant typography and harmonious color scheme
  • Graphic elements highlighting Quranic and academic themes
  • Ready-to-publish designs optimized for Instagram feed and stories

Design Process

  • Researched Islamic-inspired design aesthetics for cultural authenticity
  • Created mood boards balancing modern visuals with traditional identity
  • Designed a content system adaptable for quotes, announcements, and lessons
  • Applied brand identity elements to ensure recognition and coherence
  • Optimized designs for mobile-first viewing on Instagram

Challenges & Solutions

  • Challenge: Balancing modern visuals with Islamic cultural identity
  • Solution: Incorporated calligraphic-inspired typography and meaningful symbols within a clean design system.
  • Challenge: Keeping educational posts engaging for young audiences
  • Solution: Used vibrant layouts and consistent branding to attract attention while preserving authenticity.
