Healthcare app branding and Instagram designs for trust and credibility.

Project Overview

The Farabi Health visual identity project encompassed the complete branding strategy for a healthcare mobile application, including logo design, color palette, typography, and comprehensive Instagram content strategy. The project focused on building trust, credibility, and user engagement in the competitive healthcare market.

Key Deliverables

  • Complete visual identity for Farabi Health mobile app
  • Logo, typography, and color palette emphasizing trust and professionalism
  • Brand guidelines for digital and print applications
  • Instagram post templates promoting app features and healthcare tips
  • Consistent assets for both app interface and social media communication

Design Process

  • Researched healthcare design trends to emphasize trust and credibility
  • Created a logo and brand elements with a clean medical aesthetic (calm colors, minimal shapes)
  • Designed UI/UX identity elements ensuring brand consistency within the app
  • Developed engaging Instagram publications highlighting services and health awareness
  • Delivered a comprehensive branding package with usage guidelines

Challenges & Solutions

  • Challenge: Building trust in a competitive healthcare sector
  • Solution: Focused on clean, professional visuals with a medical-inspired color palette.
  • Challenge: Ensuring consistency between mobile app and social media identity
  • Solution: Designed a unified brand system applied across app screens and Instagram publications.
