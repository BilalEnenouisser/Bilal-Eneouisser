DivineOfficiel E-commerce (Cosmetics & Health)

E-commerce site for cosmetics and health products with COD.

Divine Agency Freelance

Project Overview

Built an e-commerce platform for DivineOfficiel to showcase and sell cosmetics and health products online. The website emphasizes trust, usability, and local accessibility with cash-on-delivery as a payment method.

Key Features

  • Product catalog with detailed descriptions and images
  • Cart and checkout with cash-on-delivery option
  • Categories for cosmetics and health products
  • SEO optimization for product visibility
  • Mobile-friendly responsive design
  • Admin dashboard for product and order management

Technical Implementation

  • WordPress CMS with WooCommerce integration
  • Custom plugins to adapt checkout flow and shipping logic
  • Tailored theme for cosmetic/health branding
  • On-page SEO setup (meta tags, structured data, optimized content)
  • Image compression and caching for performance
  • Secure hosting with SSL for safe transactions

Business Impact

  • Increased online sales by making products easily accessible
  • Enhanced brand credibility with a professional e-commerce presence
  • Simplified order handling with COD tailored to local market needs
  • Improved SEO visibility, driving organic traffic and customer acquisition
